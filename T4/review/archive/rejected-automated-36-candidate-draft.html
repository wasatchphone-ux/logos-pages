Evidence-first conditioning catalog · adult Muay Thai

Five hard minutes. Form is the limit.

For adults finishing roughly 45 minutes of demanding Muay Thai. Low explanation, minimal setup, continuous individual output. No games, teams, relays, waiting or scoring.

Near failure means approaching technical failure. Regress or stop immediately when form fails. Never work through form failure, pain, dizziness or loss of joint control.
