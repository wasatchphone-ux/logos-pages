3. Notation Decisions

Canonical technique IDs stay primary in storage and in generator logic. Numbered notation (e.g. '1-2-RK') exists only as a coach-facing display alias on top of that -- never the source of truth. Combos are stored as structured step arrays (a list of technique_id steps), not as notation strings, so the numbering can be swapped or gym-customized later without touching stored data. This is Option B from the notation proposal.

Example: {"display": "jab-cross-rear kick", "notation": "1-2-RK", "steps": [{"technique_id": "jab"}, {"technique_id": "cross"}, {"technique_id": "rear_round_kick"}]}
