Round 1
Duration: 3 minutes
Start: Both partners in stance just outside jab range.
Student: Jab only to the shown focus mitt.
Partner: Hold one head-level mitt and call ready before each jab.
Sequence: Step to range, jab, return hand to guard, reset outside range.
Trigger: Mitt appears and holder says ready.
Reset: Step back to original distance after every jab.
Stance/side: Natural stance; jab from lead hand.
Coaching focus: Straight jab path, shoulder up, hand returns to face.
Added: Opening segment only.
Switching: A works 30 seconds, B works 30 seconds, repeat three times inside the 3-minute round.
Round 2
Duration: 3 minutes
Start: Same range as Round 1.
Student: Keep Round 1 entry and add cross after the jab.
Partner: Hold two head-level mitts and keep the target still.
Sequence: Step in, jab-cross, both hands return to guard, reset outside range.
Trigger: Holder shows both mitts and says ready.
Reset: Reset after the cross before the next call.
Stance/side: Natural stance; rear heel turns on cross.
Coaching focus: Cross hip turn without leaning and guard return after two punches.
Added: Cross added to the jab entry.
Switching: A works 30 seconds, B works 30 seconds, repeat three times inside the 3-minute round.
Round 3
Duration: 3 minutes
Start: Same range, gloves at face.
Student: Retain jab-cross and answer coach calls: jab, cross, jab-cross or reset.
Partner: Hold mitts for the called hand or combo only.
Sequence: Coach call, correct punch or combo, guard return, reset.
Trigger: Coach number or word call.
Reset: Full stance reset after every call.
Stance/side: Natural stance.
Coaching focus: Listen, choose the right hand, do not abandon stance.
Added: Ludwig/Bang-style command structure, using simple WCS calls.
Switching: A works 30 seconds, B works 30 seconds, repeat three times inside the 3-minute round.
Round 4
Duration: 3 minutes
Start: Striker starts just out of range.
Student: Complete step-in jab-cross-reset challenge.
Partner: Take a small step back or angle off, then present head-level mitts.
Sequence: Find range, step in, jab-cross, step out, reset.
Trigger: Holder movement and mitt presentation.
Reset: Striker must be outside range before the next rep.
Stance/side: Natural stance.
Coaching focus: Feet move with punches; balanced finish matters more than speed.
Added: Range change added to the full jab-cross sequence.
Switching: A works 30 seconds, B works 30 seconds, repeat three times inside the 3-minute round.
