arc_id: arc-jab-cross-foundation-f53e0f7097a8   Level: Beginner   Theme: Jab and cross: mechanics through moving-distance combination

Terminal goal: By the end of Night 3, students can throw a clean jab-cross combination while moving into appropriate distance, on pads, and recover to a balanced stance.

Progression: Night 1 teaches the jab in isolation, connected immediately to entry/angling footwork. Night 2 adds the cross and combines it with the jab into the arc's core two-punch combination. Night 3 adds live reactive timing and distance-closing footwork, assembling all three nights into the arc's terminal drill: step in, jab-cross, recover to a balanced stance, ready to react again. Each night begins (from Night 2 onward) with a compressed review of the prior night's terminal skill so a student who missed a night can still participate. Each night runs Warm Up (8) -> New and Exciting (12) -> Muay Thai Curriculum (35) -> Burnout (5): an endorphin sandwich -- energetic open, focused technical teaching, hard effort close.
