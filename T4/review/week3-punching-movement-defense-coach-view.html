New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Step-to-mitt reaction relay
  • Partner changes range; striker steps and touches jab-cross to mitt.
Setup:
A: Starts outside range, steps in on B's mitt movement, touches jab-cross, steps out and circles back to start.
B: Moves the mitt to near, far or side target, then freezes long enough for a clean entry and reset.
Reps: Round 1 near/far entries, round 2 side entries, round 3 45-second clean-entry challenge per role.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: A must exit outside range before the next cue.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Touch contact only in New and Exciting.

Muay Thai Curriculum 35 min

5 min Compressed review: jab-cross on mitt
  • Jab then cross
  • Return to stance
  • Gloves on
Setup:
A: Throws jab-cross into the mitt, then returns to stance.
B: Holds one mitt at head level and calls ready before each combo.
Reps: 6 jab-crosses per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Reset to stance after every combo.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Beginner pace only; no escalation beyond clean, controlled timing.
10 min Double-jab footwork
  • Back leg drives
  • Back foot follows
  • Rear hand stays home
Setup:
Reps: Teach step rhythm, then 4 double-jab step reps, 4 step-out resets, 4 line-entry reps, and a 60-second coach-call entry challenge.
Sides: natural stance unless otherwise stated
Self-contained teach; keep only the listed beginner portion of the source.
20 min Terminal step-in/step-out jab-cross
  • Feet move with punches
  • Do not lean
  • Reset after the cross
Setup:
A: Begins just out of range, steps in with jab-cross, then steps back out to guard.
B: Holds the mitt at head level, then takes a small step back after each reset to make A find range again.
Reps: 2 rounds of 8 step-in jab-crosses per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: A must be back out of range before the next rep starts.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Beginner pace only; no escalation beyond clean, controlled timing.

Burnout 5 min

5 min Bodyweight core sprint circuit
  • Close class with controlled high intensity.
Setup:
Reps: 5 rounds of 40 seconds work / 20 seconds rest, alternating sprawls and bicycle crunches.
Sides: symmetric
Format: 5 rounds of 40 seconds work / 20 seconds rest, alternating sprawls and bicycle crunches.
Coach calls: Coach runs the clock, calls every work/rest/switch interval, and counts down the final 10 seconds.
Scaling: Reduce speed, remove jumps, or shorten the strike burst while keeping the same interval structure.
Technique stays controlled under fatigue; students stop if dizzy or unable to hold form.

New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Parry number-call game
  • Partner calls jab or cross; defender performs the matching light parry shape.
Setup:
A: Makes the called parry shape, returns to guard, then adds jab-touch to mitt after clean reactions in the final round.
B: Calls one or two and feeds a slow glove touch; final round adds mitt target after A parries.
Reps: Round 1 dry parry calls, round 2 slow glove feeds, round 3 parry-plus-jab-touch challenge.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: Hands return to guard after every parry or touch.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Slow glove touch only.

Setup:
A: Steps in, throws jab-cross to the mitt, steps out.
B: Holds the mitt at head level and moves back one step between reps.
Reps: 5 clean moving jab-crosses per student.

Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair

12 min Parrying straight punches
  • Small parry
  • Eyes open
  • Return hand to guard
Setup:
A: Parries announced jabs and crosses, resets, then adds jab-cross return to mitt for the final set.
B: Feeds announced straight punches slowly, then presents mitt only after A makes a clean parry.
Reps: Set 1 jab parries, set 2 cross parries, set 3 mixed announced parries, set 4 parry-and-return challenge.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Both partners reset to guard after each feed.
Equipment: Boxing gloves
Announced straight punches only.
18 min Terminal parry and return jab-cross
  • Parry small
  • Return immediately
  • Reset after return
Setup:
A: Parries B's announced straight punch, then immediately returns jab-cross to the mitt.
B: Feeds one slow straight punch, then presents the mitt for A's return.
Reps: 2 rounds of 6 parry-and-return reps per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Reset outside range after the jab-cross return.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
No surprise feeds.

New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Block-and-go reaction lane
  • Defender blocks a light announced feed, returns to mitt, and exits the lane.
Setup:
A: Blocks the glove feed on high guard, returns jab-cross to mitt, then exits one step left or right.
B: Feeds one straight glove touch, then holds the mitt for the return.
Reps: 3 rounds of 30 seconds per role.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: After the exit step, both partners return to the lane start.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Straight feeds only.

10 min High-guard block against straight punches
  • Hands in front of face
  • Elbows in
  • Do not turn away
Setup:
A: Blocks the announced straight punch, resets eyes and guard, then returns jab-cross to mitt in the final set.
B: Feeds slow straight punches and presents the mitt for returns only after clean blocks.
Reps: Set 1 block shape, set 2 moving block, set 3 block-and-return, set 4 45-second clean-block challenge.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Both reset to range after every block or return.
Equipment: Boxing gloves
Straight feeds only; no surprise combinations.
20 min Terminal block, jab-cross return, step out
  • Block first
  • Return immediately
  • Move out after the return
Setup:
A: Blocks the straight feed, returns jab-cross to the mitt, then steps out at an angle.
B: Feeds one straight punch to guard, presents the mitt for the return, then lets A exit.
Reps: 2 rounds of 6 block-return-exit reps per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: After the exit step, both partners return to starting distance.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Announced feed, controlled power.
