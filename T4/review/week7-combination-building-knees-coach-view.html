Objective: Connect punches to kicks without overlong combinations.

New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Coach-call two-tool combo game
  • Coach calls hands or kick finish; pair builds the called two-tool combo.
Setup:
A: Builds the called short combo, resets, then races for clean coach-called chains in the final round.
B: Presents mitt and shield targets in the called order, pausing until A fully resets.
Reps: Round 1 hands-to-kick, round 2 hands-to-teep, round 3 mixed call challenge per role.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: Both reset after each two-tool combo.
Equipment: Boxing gloves, 1 focus mitt and 1 kicking shield per pair
Targets only; no free-choice long combinations.

Muay Thai Curriculum 35 min

5 min Compressed review: jab-cross-teep or kick
  • Two punches
  • One kick finish
  • Reset
Setup:
A: Throws jab-cross to mitt, then one called teep or kick to shield.
B: Presents mitts for jab-cross, then braces the shield for the called finish.
Reps: 5 short combos per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Reset to guard after the shield finish.
Equipment: Boxing gloves, 1 focus mitt and 1 kicking shield per pair
Beginner pace only; no escalation beyond clean, controlled timing.
12 min Two punches into kick
  • Do not rush the kick
  • Reset after finish
  • Holder braces shield
Setup:
A: Throws two straight punches to mitts, pauses long enough to stay balanced, then kicks the shield.
B: Feeds mitts for the two punches, lowers or turns the shield for the kick, and checks that A resets.
Reps: 2 rounds of 6 punch-to-kick reps per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: A lands the kick and returns to stance before the next rep.
Equipment: Boxing gloves, 1 focus mitt and 1 kicking shield per pair
Holder braces before the kick.
18 min Terminal jab-cross-kick combination
  • Hands set up kick
  • Kick lands on shield
  • Reset immediately
Setup:
A: Runs jab-cross to mitts and one shielded kick as a clean three-part chain.
B: Holds mitts for jab-cross, then presents the kicking shield without adding extra calls.
Reps: 2 rounds of 6 three-part combinations per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Full reset after the kick.
Equipment: Boxing gloves, 1 focus mitt and 1 kicking shield per pair
Three parts maximum.

New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Marching-knee count relay
  • Partner holds shield; striker alternates marching knee drives for a count.
Setup:
A: Alternates marching knees, then adds step-in straight knees and a short count challenge.
B: Braces shield for marching knees first, then steps back one pace for the step-in version.
Reps: Round 1 marching count, round 2 step-in knee, round 3 30-second clean-knee count challenge per role.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: A steps back to stance after each burst.
Equipment: 1 kicking shield per pair
No clinch grip or pulling on the holder.

10 min Straight knee to shield
  • Make space
  • Knee up the middle
  • Posture tall
Setup:
A: Steps in, drives one straight knee up the middle into the shield, steps back to stance.
B: Holds the shield square at belly height and stays planted until A steps back.
Reps: 2 rounds of 8 straight knees per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: A steps back out to stance after every knee.
Equipment: 1 kicking shield per pair
No clinch grip; hands may post lightly on the shield only.
10 min Terminal jab-cross-straight-knee
  • Hands first
  • Knee straight up the middle
  • No clinch grip

B: Holds mitts for jab-cross, then presents the shield and stays still for the knee.
Reps: 2 rounds of 5 jab-cross-knee reps per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Reset outside knee range after every sequence.
Equipment: Boxing gloves, 1 focus mitt and 1 kicking shield per pair
No clinch control or extra knees.
