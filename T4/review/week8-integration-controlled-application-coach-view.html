New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Tool-card target relay
  • Coach shows a tool card; pair responds with the matching already-taught target action.
Setup:
A: Responds to the card with one clean target action, then resets for the next card.
B: Shows one card at a time, presents the matching mitt or shield, and speeds up only after clean resets.
Reps: Round 1 hands/teep cards, round 2 kick/knee cards, round 3 mixed-tool target challenge per role.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: One card, one response, full reset.
Equipment: Boxing gloves, shin guards, 1 focus mitt and 1 kicking shield per pair
Targets only.

  • Reset
Setup:

10 min Predictable combination defense
  • Predictable feed
  • Defense first
  • Eyes open
Setup:
A: Defends a known short combination, returns one target strike, then exits to stance.
B: Feeds the known order slowly first, then adds coach-called return targets in the final set.
Reps: Set 1 dry defense path, set 2 predictable feed, set 3 defend-and-return, set 4 clean-exit challenge.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Reset after the return lands.
Equipment: Boxing gloves
Known order only.
20 min Terminal toolkit target round
  • One call at a time
  • Capped power
  • Reset every exchange
Setup:
A: Responds to coach call with one clean tool: jab-cross, teep, rear kick, low kick, knee, block-return or check-return.
B: Presents the matching mitt or shield target and keeps the round moving.
Reps: 3 rounds of 60 seconds, switching roles each round.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: One call, one response, reset.
Equipment: Boxing gloves, shin guards, 1 focus mitt and 1 kicking shield per pair
Targets only.

Burnout 5 min

5 min Solo all-body sprint circuit
  • Close class with controlled high intensity.
Setup:
Reps: 5 rounds of 40 seconds work / 20 seconds rest, rotating burpees, squats, mountain climbers, line hops and sprawls.
Sides: symmetric
Format: 5 rounds of 40 seconds work / 20 seconds rest, rotating burpees, squats, mountain climbers, line hops and sprawls.
Coach calls: Coach runs the clock, calls every work/rest/switch interval, and counts down the final 10 seconds.
Scaling: Reduce speed, remove jumps, or shorten the strike burst while keeping the same interval structure.
Technique stays controlled under fatigue; students stop if dizzy or unable to hold form.

12 min Coach-refereed choice rehearsal
  • Pairs rehearse constrained choices before the formal Curriculum sequence.
Setup:
A: Chooses the correct already-taught defense or target response on the coach call, executes once, then freezes.
