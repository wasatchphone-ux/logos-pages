New and Exciting 12 min

12 min FightClub force-transfer jab-cross pad challenge
  • Use FightClub's power-transfer idea as a target-based jab-cross application after the warm-up.
Setup:
A: Gloves on. A throws controlled jab-cross into head-level mitts, feeling foot pressure, hip turn, and guard return after every combination.
B: B holds focus mitts at head level, calls ready, and gives simple feedback: straight line, clean return, balanced finish.
Reps: Four 3-minute progressive rounds: jab; jab-cross; number-call jab/cross/reset; step-in jab-cross-reset.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: Reset to full stance after each cue before the next cue.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and focus mitts
