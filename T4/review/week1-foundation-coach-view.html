Night 1 -- Stance, Guard, Jab and Cross

Level: Beginner
Objective: Learn a Muay Thai stance and guard while throwing jab, cross and jab-cross on targets.
Equipment checklist: 10 focus mitts, Boxing gloves
Format: Partner pairs required
Space: 10 pairs with focus mitts; enough lane space for step-in and reset reps.

Warm Up 8 min

Warm-up menu: use these six-plus options, combine or trim based on the room while keeping the block exactly 8 minutes.

1.0 min Jumping jacks
  • Raise heart rate quickly.
Setup:
Reps: 45 seconds continuous
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min High knees in place
  • Build heat and knee drive.
Setup:
Reps: 45 seconds continuous
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min Fast feet
  • Prime light feet.
Setup:
Reps: 45 seconds continuous
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min Bodyweight squats
  • Warm hips and legs.
Setup:
Reps: 12 controlled reps
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min Line hops
  • Add direction changes without technique load.
Setup:
Reps: 30 seconds forward/back, 30 seconds lateral
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min Jab-cross base check
  • Find stance, guard and balance before class-specific work.
Setup:
Reps: 3 holds of 5 seconds
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min Jab-cross movement primer
  • Preview the movement path used in Curriculum.
Setup:
Reps: 8 slow reps
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min Reset-to-stance check
  • Make recovery to stance automatic.
Setup:
Reps: 6 resets on coach clap
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side

New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Source drill: jab-cross target starter
  • Use Easton focus-mitt jab-cross work as the first recognizable Muay Thai target contact of the class.
Setup:
A: Gloves on. A starts in stance, touches jab-cross to the mitt, then resets outside range.
B: B holds one head-level focus mitt and calls ready before each rep.
Reps: Phase 1 stance-to-target, Phase 2 jab-cross reps, Phase 3 step-in jab-cross challenge.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: Reset to full stance after each cue before the next cue.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
No surprise full-power contact; the feeder must be set before the response.

Muay Thai Curriculum 35 min

8 min Muay Thai stance and guard for punching
  • Front foot points more square than boxing
  • Hands at eyebrow level
  • Elbows slightly out so blocks are available
Setup:
Reps: Phase 1: stance/guard teach. Phase 2: guard return after each shoulder tap. Phase 3: ready stance before mitt work.
Sides: natural stance unless otherwise stated
Self-contained teach; keep only the listed beginner portion of the source.
8 min Jab and cross mechanics
  • Hand leads from the face
  • Retract punches back to the face
  • For cross, turn the heel to activate hip
Setup:
Reps: Phase 1: jab reps. Phase 2: cross reps. Phase 3: jab-cross shadow reps with guard return.
Sides: natural stance unless otherwise stated
Self-contained teach; keep only the listed beginner portion of the source.
8 min Jab-cross on focus mitts
  • Shoulder up on the punch
  • Good extension
  • Return to stance after the combination
Setup:
A: Performs the active skill sequence, then resets to stance before the next rep.
B: Feeds or holds the target exactly as assigned, then resets before the next rep.
Reps: 2 rounds of 6-8 clean reps per student, adjusted by coach pace
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Both partners reset to full stance after every rep or exchange.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Beginner pace only; no escalation beyond clean, controlled timing.
11 min Terminal step-in jab-cross and reset
  • Drive off the back leg
  • Bring the back foot with you
  • Return back to original distance

Burnout 5 min

5 min Solo punch-conditioner burnout
  • Close class with controlled high intensity.
Setup:
Reps: 5 rounds of 40 seconds work / 20 seconds rest: fast straight punches in guard, squats, mountain climbers, fast straight punches, line hops.
Sides: symmetric
Format: 5 rounds of 40 seconds work / 20 seconds rest: fast straight punches in guard, squats, mountain climbers, fast straight punches, line hops.
Coach calls: Coach runs the clock, calls every work/rest/switch interval, and counts down the final 10 seconds.
Scaling: Reduce speed, remove jumps, or shorten the strike burst while keeping the same interval structure.
Technique stays controlled under fatigue; students stop if dizzy or unable to hold form.
