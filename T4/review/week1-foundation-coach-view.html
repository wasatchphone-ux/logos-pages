Night 1 -- Stance, Guard and Balance

Level: Beginner
Objective: Hold stance and guard while moving and resetting.
Equipment checklist: 10 focus mitts, Boxing gloves, open floor lanes
Format: Partner pairs required
Space: Open floor, 4 rows of 5, then 10 partner lanes for reaction work.

Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side

  • Build heat and knee drive.
Setup:

New and Exciting 12 min

4 min Mirror guard freeze
  • Quick mirror-and-freeze game to make stance live without stretching it.
Setup:
A: Leads tiny stance steps and freezes on coach clap.
B: Mirrors and freezes in guard; if stance breaks, both reset.
Reps: 4 x 30-second rounds total, switching leaders after each round.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: Reset to full stance after each rep, cue, or target touch.
Equipment: open floor lanes
Beginner pace; touch targets only when a target is assigned.
4 min Guard target touch
  • Add one beginner-safe Muay Thai stimulus: touch the mitt from guard, then reset.
Setup:
A: Holds guard, touches the mitt with a light jab when B shows it, then resets.
B: Presents one focus mitt at head level only after A is still in stance.
Reps: 3 x 35-second touch rounds per role, fast switches.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: Reset to full stance after each rep, cue, or target touch.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Beginner pace; touch targets only when a target is assigned.
4 min Freeze-touch team race
  • Short competitive finish: move, freeze, touch target, reset clean.
Setup:
A: Takes one step on coach call, freezes, touches the mitt, and returns to stance.
B: Holds the mitt still and counts only clean step-freeze-touch-reset reps.
Reps: 2 x 45-second rounds per role; clean reps only.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: Reset to full stance after each rep, cue, or target touch.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Beginner pace; touch targets only when a target is assigned.

Muay Thai Curriculum 35 min

8 min Muay Thai stance and guard
  • Feet under hips
  • Hands return to face
  • Soft knees and eyes forward
Setup:
Reps: Phase 1: 90-second stance teach. Phase 2: 3 x 20-second guard holds. Phase 3: 6 glove-to-guard returns. Phase 4: 60-second balance-reset challenge.
Sides: natural stance unless otherwise stated
Self-contained teach; keep only the listed beginner portion of the source.
8 min Basic movement: forward, back and lateral
  • Lead with the foot in the direction you move
  • Small steps
  • Reset after the touch
Setup:
Reps: Phase 1: 90-second step teach. Phase 2: 4 coach-call steps each direction. Phase 3: 6 step-and-jab-touch reps to a held mitt. Phase 4: 60-second direction-call challenge.
Sides: natural stance unless otherwise stated
Self-contained teach; keep only the listed beginner portion of the source.
8 min Guard return after touch
  • Touch, do not punch hard
  • Hands return to face
  • Feet stay under hips
Setup:
A: Phase 1: touches the mitt from stance. Phase 2: touches then returns both hands to guard. Phase 3: adds one small step before the touch.
B: Holds one focus mitt at head level, calls 'ready,' and counts only clean touch-and-guard reps.
Reps: A: 6 touch-return reps, switch. A+B: 6 step-touch-return reps, switch. A+B+C: 45-second clean-rep challenge per role.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Reset to full stance after each phase or target touch.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Touch targets only; no sparring, chasing, or power punching.
11 min Terminal stance-to-target ladder
  • See the cue
  • Freeze before touching
  • Exit and reset
Setup:
A: A: move on B's cue and freeze in guard. A+B: move, freeze, touch the mitt. A+B+C: move, freeze, touch, exit back to range and reset.
B: Feeds one clear movement cue, holds a head-level mitt only after A freezes, then checks the reset.
Reps: A: 6 cue-and-freeze reps per role. A+B: 6 cue-freeze-touch reps per role. A+B+C: 2 x 45-second scored rounds per role.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Reset to full stance after each phase or target touch.
Equipment: Boxing gloves and 1 focus mitt per pair
Touch targets only; no sparring, chasing, or power punching.

Burnout 5 min

5 min Solo footwork sprint intervals
  • Close class with controlled high intensity.
Setup:
Reps: 5 rounds of 40 seconds fast feet / 20 seconds rest.
Sides: symmetric
Format: 5 rounds of 40 seconds fast feet / 20 seconds rest.
Coach calls: Coach runs the clock, calls every work/rest/switch interval, and counts down the final 10 seconds.
Scaling: Reduce speed, remove jumps, or shorten the strike burst while keeping the same interval structure.
Technique stays controlled under fatigue; students stop if dizzy or unable to hold form.
