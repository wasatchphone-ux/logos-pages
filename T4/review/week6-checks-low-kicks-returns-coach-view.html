Night 1 -- Low Kick Placement and Arc 1 Bridge

Level: Beginner
Objective: Review rear kick, then place basic low kicks safely.
Equipment checklist: 10 kicking shields, shin guards
Format: Partner pairs required
Space: 10 pairs with shields; no live low kicks to unprotected legs.

Warm-up menu: use these six-plus options, combine or trim based on the room while keeping the block exactly 8 minutes.

Reps: 45 seconds continuous
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min High knees to check
  • Build heat and knee drive.
Setup:
Reps: 45 seconds continuous
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min Fast-feet low-kick call
  • Prime light feet.
Setup:

Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min Squat-to-guard
  • Warm hips and legs.
Setup:
Reps: 12 controlled reps
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side

  • Add direction changes without technique load.
Setup:
Reps: 30 seconds forward/back, 30 seconds lateral
Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side
1.0 min Low kick base check
  • Find stance, guard and balance before class-specific work.
Setup:

Sides: symmetric unless the coach assigns stance side

New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Shield target low-kick accuracy game
  • Students touch marked shield zones for outside and inside low-kick targets.
Setup:
A: Touches the called low-kick shield zone, resets, then answers faster zone changes in the final round.
B: Holds the shield low and calls outside, inside or reset, changing rhythm only after A is balanced.
Reps: Round 1 zone teach, round 2 moving target, round 3 45-second accuracy challenge per role.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: A resets to stance after each kick before B calls the next zone.
Equipment: 1 kicking shield per pair
Shield contact only; no kicks to legs in this game.

Muay Thai Curriculum 35 min

5 min Compressed review: rear round kick to shield
  • Same rear kick mechanics
  • Recover to stance
  • Shield only
Setup:
A: Throws the rear round kick to the shield, lands balanced, and returns to guard.
B: Braces the shield at body height and gives a ready call before each kick.
Reps: 6 clean rear kicks per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Both reset to starting range after every kick.
Equipment: 1 kicking shield per pair
Beginner pace only; no escalation beyond clean, controlled timing.
12 min Outside low kick targeting
  • Target lead-leg line
  • Shin through shield
  • Recover stance
Setup:
A: Places low kicks on the shield, then adds range step and jab-cross entry before the low kick in the final set.
B: Holds the shield low, then starts A just outside range for the entry version.
Reps: Set 1 placement, set 2 range step, set 3 jab-cross entry to low kick, set 4 clean target challenge.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: A lands and recovers to guard before the next rep.
Equipment: 1 kicking shield per pair
Target is the shield only, never an unprotected leg.
18 min Terminal rear kick to outside low kick
  • Same kick base
  • Change level to low kick
  • Reset after both
Setup:
A: Kicks the body-height shield once, resets, then changes level into one outside low kick on the low shield.
B: Presents body-height shield for the first kick, lowers to the lead-leg line after A resets, then braces for the low kick.
Reps: 2 rounds of 5 two-kick sequences per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Full stance reset between the rear kick and the low kick.
Equipment: 1 kicking shield per pair
Holder must be braced before each kick.

Burnout 5 min

5 min Low-kick shield burst
  • Close class with controlled high intensity.
Setup:
A: Works during the assigned interval.
B: Holds or rests as assigned, then switches.
Reps: 4 rounds of 75 seconds: 30 seconds shield low-kick burst, switch, 30 seconds partner burst, reset.
Switch: Switch on the coach call.
Reset: Reset at each interval boundary.
Equipment: 1 kicking shield per pair
Format: 4 rounds of 75 seconds: 30 seconds shield low-kick burst, switch, 30 seconds partner burst, reset.
Coach calls: Coach runs the clock, calls every work/rest/switch interval, and counts down the final 10 seconds.
Scaling: Reduce speed, remove jumps, or shorten the strike burst while keeping the same interval structure.
Technique stays controlled under fatigue; students stop if dizzy or unable to hold form.

New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Check-call reaction ladder
  • Partner shows outside or inside cue; defender lifts the matching check shape.
Setup:
A: Reads outside or inside cue, makes the check, resets, then adds shield low-kick return in the final round.
B: Feeds visual cues first, then presents the shield after clean checks for the return round.
Reps: Round 1 dry cues, round 2 faster cues, round 3 check-and-return target challenge per role.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: A sets the checking foot down under the hip before the next cue.
Equipment: shin guards
Cue-only until the return target round; no live hard kicks.

Muay Thai Curriculum 35 min

5 min Compressed review: outside low kick to shield
  • Shield only
  • Recover stance
  • Do not chase
Setup:
A: Places one outside low kick on the shield, then recovers to guard.
B: Holds the shield on the lead-leg line and gives a ready call before each rep.
Reps: 5 clean shield low kicks per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Both reset to kicking range after every rep.
Equipment: 1 kicking shield per pair
Beginner pace only; no escalation beyond clean, controlled timing.
12 min Outside and cross check
  • Knee lifts to check
  • Base stays strong
  • Hands stay high
Setup:
A: Checks the announced line, lands balanced, then returns one low kick to the shield in the final set.
B: Feeds one announced slow low kick line, then presents shield after clean checks.
Reps: Set 1 outside checks, set 2 cross checks, set 3 mixed announced checks, set 4 check-return challenge.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Reset to guard after every feed.
Equipment: shin guards
Slow announced feeds only; no sweeps, catches or follow-up counter systems.
18 min Terminal check and return low kick
  • Check first
  • Return only to shield
  • Reset after exchange
Setup:
A: Checks B's slow low-kick feed, lands in stance, then returns one outside low kick to the shield.
B: Feeds one slow low kick, then immediately raises the shield on the lead-leg line for A's return.
Reps: 2 rounds of 6 check-and-return reps per student.
Switch: After Partner A finishes the assigned reps, partners switch roles; repeat for round 2.
Reset: Both partners reset after the shield return.
Equipment: shin guards and 1 kicking shield per pair
Return goes to shield only.

New and Exciting 12 min

12 min Two-card defense reaction game
  • Feeder shows hand or kick cue; defender performs the matching block/check shape.
Setup:
A: Reads hand or shin card, makes the defense, then returns to the shown mitt or shield target in the final round.
B: Shows one cue at a time and presents the correct target only after A's clean defense.
Reps: Round 1 visual defense, round 2 slow feed, round 3 defend-and-return target challenge per role.
Switch: After each 30-second round, partners switch roles.
Reset: A returns to full guard before the next card.
Equipment: Boxing gloves, shin guards, 1 focus mitt and 1 kicking shield per pair
Announced cues only.
