Conflicts with Curriculum?none

Partner bHolds a focus mitt and, on the coach's call, takes one step back to open the distance, then holds still for Partner A's reaction.
Equipment1 focus mitt per pair (10 mitts for a class of 20).
Contact levelLight touch/tap only, constrained movement -- full-power technique is taught on pads in Curriculum.
RepsRound 1 near/far entry calls, round 2 side target calls, round 3 45-second clean-entry challenge per role.
