P01 · legs

Quad Lockdown

STATUS: APPROVE: Approved for in-person pilot testing

Coaching cue: Ten thirty-second blocks. Move immediately at each call. Regress at the first loss of the stated form standard and keep working cleanly.

  1. 0:00–0:30 Wall sit with hips approximately level with knees.
  2. 0:30–1:00 Controlled bodyweight squats at moderate pace.
  3. 1:00–1:30 Wall sit with hips approximately level with knees, sustained hold.
  4. 1:30–2:00 Bodyweight squats at controlled pace.
  5. 2:00–2:30 Wall sit with hips approximately level with knees, hold.
  6. 2:30–3:00 Squat pulses in bottom third of range.
  7. 3:00–3:30 Wall sit with hips approximately level with knees. If posture, knee position or back contact begins to deteriorate, slide higher and continue holding.
  8. 3:30–4:00 Fast bodyweight squats while maintaining controlled depth and knee tracking; regress to moderate pace if form begins to slip.
  9. 4:00–4:30 Squat pulses in bottom third of range.
  10. 4:30–5:00 Squat pulses in bottom third of range; when depth begins to shorten, switch to quarter-squat pulses in the shallow range. If quarter-pulses become uncontrolled, hold the shallow position for the remaining time.
Target
Concentrated quadriceps local muscular endurance through alternating wall sits, full-range squats and shortened-range squat pulses
Progression
Maintain 90-degree wall sit depth; increase squat cadence; extend pulse-work duration.
Evidence
Coach-designed sequence using sourced exercises and programming principles
