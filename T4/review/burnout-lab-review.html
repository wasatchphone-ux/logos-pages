Retained source activity 1 · R2-009

Four-move floor-core circuit

Muay Thai Finisher Ab Workout Circuit · 2:01-3:00

Observed structure

Double crunches, Russian twists, alternating knee extensions and flutter kicks; source recommends 3-5 sets.

Roles and transitions

All athletes work together; coach calls the change.

Five-minute adaptation

Use fixed short intervals and remove the source's open-ended set count.

Demand and equipment

Moderate-to-high trunk endurance; Mats optional

Burden and difficulty

Low; Low

Safety and beginner fit

Stop for low-back pain; keep lumbar control. Good with regressions
