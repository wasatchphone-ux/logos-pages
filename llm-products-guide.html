The LLM Product
Landscape

A corrected, consolidated guide to major AI products, surfaces, and best use cases across Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, xAI, and Meta. Focused on actual interfaces and workspaces — not just raw model versions.

Claude
Anthropic
→ claude.ai

Strongest for structured, agentic workspaces. The Projects + Artifacts + Code stack is the benchmark for autonomous creation workflows.

Claude.ai
Main Interface
Best for: General problem-solving, writing, research, data analysis, PDF/image analysis. Free tier includes search, file handling, and memory. Pro/Max unlocks extended thinking and heavier models.
Projects
Persistent Workspace
Best for: Organizing long-running work with persistent context and custom knowledge bases. Upload style guides, docs, and APIs — every chat in a Project inherits the rules. Ideal for teams, multi-week initiatives, or client separation.
Artifacts
Live Output Panel
Best for: Creating and iterating on interactive outputs — code previews, SVG graphics, web pages, charts, and apps — rendered live in a side panel without leaving the chat. Essential for rapid prototyping.
Claude Code
Agentic Coding · CLI + IDE
Best for: Autonomous, multi-file software engineering. Reads codebases, plans changes, writes, tests, and debugs iteratively until done. Integrates with VS Code, JetBrains, and terminal. Supports parallel agents and routines.
Claude Cowork
Desktop Agent · Research Preview
Best for: Non-technical desktop knowledge work — browser, spreadsheets, file management — via agents. The non-dev counterpart to Claude Code. Sustains workflows across apps without manual hand-holding.
Claude Design
Design Agent · Anthropic Labs
Best for: Prototyping slides, one-pagers, and UI from design systems. Turn briefs into visual deliverables fast. Launched April 17, 2026 via Anthropic Labs.
Claude in Chrome
Browser Agent · Beta
Best for: AI assistance embedded directly in your browser session — summarizing pages, drafting replies, researching inline without context-switching.
Claude in Excel
Spreadsheet Agent · Beta
Best for: Spreadsheet automation, formula generation, data cleaning, and analysis directly inside Excel without leaving the file.
Claude in PowerPoint
Slides Agent · Beta
Best for: Drafting, restructuring, and editing presentations from inside PowerPoint. Generates slide content and structure from prompts or existing documents.
Claude for Slack
Messaging Integration
Best for: AI assistance directly inside Slack — summarizing threads, drafting replies, and answering questions without leaving the communication layer. Brings Claude into team workflows where decisions actually happen.
Claude for Word
Document Agent · Beta
Best for: Drafting, editing, and restructuring documents directly inside Microsoft Word. Part of the same office integration suite as Claude in Excel and Claude in PowerPoint.
ChatGPT
OpenAI
→ chatgpt.com

Strongest for ecosystem breadth. The Projects + Deep Research + Codex stack is highly steerable and covers both everyday use and professional agentic workflows. Enterprise connectors make it the default for team deployments.

ChatGPT
Main Interface
Best for: General writing, research, nuanced language tasks, creative generation, and broad daily use. Supports memory, multimodal input, and custom instructions. The most widely deployed LLM consumer surface.
Projects
Persistent Workspace
Best for: Long-running mixed work with files, shared context, custom instructions, and persistent memory across sessions. Direct OpenAI equivalent to Claude's Projects.
Deep Research
Agentic Research Workflow
Best for: Citation-heavy web research and synthesis. Runs multi-step research across the web, can use uploaded files and enabled apps, and returns structured reports. Distinct from standard chat — its own agentic loop.
GPTs
Custom Assistants
Best for: Building repeatable custom assistants with specific instructions, knowledge bases, and capabilities. Shareable with teams or the public via the GPT store.
Apps in ChatGPT
External Tool Platform
Best for: Turning ChatGPT into a front-end for external tools and specialized workflows. Built via the Apps SDK — a distinct platform from GPTs, enabling richer integrations and interactivity.
Tasks
Scheduled Automations
Best for: One-time or recurring scheduled automations inside ChatGPT. Automate repetitive prompts, reminders, or data pulls on a defined cadence.
Codex
Agentic Coding · App + CLI
Best for: Parallel coding agents and long-running engineering work. Steerable while running, strong on terminal and OS-level tasks. Pro tier targets heavy dev users. Separate from the deprecated Codex API.
o-series (o3 / o3-pro)
Reasoning Models
Best for: Deep reasoning and reliable high-stakes responses. Extended internal thinking before answering — best for math, logic, scientific analysis, and any task where accuracy outweighs speed.
Apps in ChatGPT
Integrations + External Tools
Best for: Connecting ChatGPT to external services — Drive, SharePoint, GitHub, Teams, Gmail, Jira, Confluence — and building specialized workflows via the Apps SDK. OpenAI has unified connectors and apps under this single surface. Core driver of enterprise business value beyond raw chat.
ChatGPT Desktop + Mobile
Native App Surfaces
Best for: Persistent access to ChatGPT outside the browser — desktop app for macOS/Windows with system-level integration, mobile for on-the-go use. Distinct product pages and download flows from OpenAI; relevant for teams evaluating deployment surfaces.
Gemini
Google
→ gemini.google.com

Strongest on speed, native multimodality, and Google ecosystem integration. NotebookLM has emerged as a standalone research product with its own user base.

Gemini
Main Interface
Best for: Multimodal workflows — image, video, and text synthesis at high speed. Integrates natively with YouTube, Google Docs, Drive, and Gmail. Often leads in low-latency scenarios and granular control.
Canvas
Interactive Output Builder
Best for: Turning prompts or Deep Research outputs into interactive artifacts — apps, games, quizzes, web pages, and infographics. Fast prototyping from idea to shareable product.
Gems
Custom AI Experts
Best for: Building custom repeatable role-based AI assistants within Gemini. Define a persona, instructions, and context — then reuse across conversations. Google's equivalent of GPTs.
Deep Research
Agentic Research Workflow
Best for: Multi-step web research with source attribution, used as a dedicated research mode inside Gemini. Produces structured reports with citations, connected directly to Google Search infrastructure.
NotebookLM
Source-Grounded Workspace
Best for: Research, study, and synthesis grounded strictly in your uploaded sources. Supports notebooks, source-based chat, public sharing, Audio Overviews (podcast-style summaries), and Video Overviews. Increasingly integrated with Gemini.
Personal Intelligence
Personalized Context Layer
Best for: AI assistance that uses your personal data — calendar, emails, history — for contextually aware help. Useful for scheduling, personal planning, and cross-app synthesis for individual users.
Gemini in Chrome
Browser Integration
Best for: Embedded AI assistance directly in the Chrome browser. Summarize pages, draft text, answer questions inline without leaving your current tab.
Grok
xAI
→ grok.com

Strongest on real-time knowledge (X platform integration), domain reliability, and lower censorship. Grok Business adds team governance for enterprise contexts.

Grok
Main Interface
Best for: Real-time information via X integration, domain expertise (medicine, legal), reasoning + speed, and tasks requiring minimal content filtering. Also accessible via x.com and mobile apps.
Grok Business / Workspaces
Team Platform
Best for: Teams that need Grok inside managed, governed collaboration environments. Includes workspace-level controls, team sharing, and admin features for enterprise deployments.
Grok Apps / Connectors
External Integrations
Best for: Connecting Grok to external data sources, including Google Drive integration. Enables document-grounded conversations and workflow connections beyond the main chat surface.
Grok Imagine
Image & Video Generation
Best for: Image generation and editing within the Grok ecosystem. Relevant if comparing full multimodal product suites rather than text-only capabilities. Video generation available in supported tiers.
Llama / Meta AI
Meta
→ ai.meta.com

Strongest for self-hosted, customizable deployments where privacy, control, and compute economics matter more than turnkey UX. Meta AI covers the consumer surface across Meta's platforms.

Meta AI
Consumer Assistant
Best for: General assistance across Meta's consumer platforms — WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Messenger, and web. The consumer product surface, distinct from the open-weight model releases. Widest potential reach of any AI assistant by platform footprint.
Meta AI Studio
Creator Platform
Best for: Building and deploying custom AI personas and assistants across Meta's platforms. Distinct from Meta AI — targeted at creators, businesses, and developers who want to build AI-powered presences inside Meta's ecosystem.
Vibes
Multimodal Creation · Consumer
Best for: AI-generated images and videos through a dedicated consumer creation surface. Separate from the main Meta AI assistant — functions as Meta's answer to standalone creative generation tools.
Llama 4 Maverick
Open-Weight · Large MoE
Best for: General assistant tasks, creative writing, coding, reasoning, multilingual, and multimodal (text + image) when self-hosted. Competitive with closed models on many benchmarks at significantly lower cost at scale. Strong performance-to-cost ratio for orgs with infra.
Llama 4 Scout
Open-Weight · Efficient
Best for: Long-context processing (up to 10M tokens), document and codebase summarization, and efficient multimodal on constrained hardware. Fits on a single H100 GPU. Ideal for self-hosted RAG pipelines or massive-input workflows.
Self-Hosted / Fine-Tuned
Deployment Approach
Best for: Organizations that need data privacy, domain-specific fine-tuning, zero per-token cost, or full model control. Llama weights are openly available — the right path for SMEs with GPU infrastructure or specialized vertical applications.
