How to read the tiers

  • A: possible asymmetry with unusually wide outcomes.
  • B: attractive conventional return potential; 3x not required.
  • C: viable setup or watch, not a rejection list.

Evidence discipline

Primary-source operating evidence drives advancement. Technical structure only refines entry quality. Historical highs are not treated as intrinsic value. Missing or contradictory evidence is stated rather than filled with assumptions.
